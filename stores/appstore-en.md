Rawdah — your personal guide to learning and memorizing the 99 Beautiful Names of Allah (Asmaul Husna).

Listen to native speaker pronunciation of each name. Learn to recite flawlessly.

Test your knowledge with engaging quizzes. Multiple choice and true/false questions make learning effective.

◆ Progress Tracking
Watch your growth. Statistics show how many names you've mastered and your learning journey.

◆ Gamification
• Earn XP for every lesson

• Unlock achievements and badges

99 names organized into 11 themed sections with 9 names each. Every name includes Arabic text, transliteration, meaning, and audio pronunciation.

Learn anywhere — all content works without internet connection.

◆ Dark & Light Themes
Choose your comfortable viewing mode for any time of day.
